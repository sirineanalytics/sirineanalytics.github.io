About

Data-Driven Marketer Turned Analytics Specialist

I started my career on the brand side — running paid social campaigns, optimizing landing pages, and chasing ROAS targets. But I kept hitting the same wall: we were making decisions based on incomplete data and biased attribution.

That frustration led me deep into marketing analytics. Today, I help DTC brands build measurement systems that actually reflect reality — from automated dashboards to causal MMM models that account for incrementality.

My clients are typically scaling DTC brands ($5M–$50M ARR) who have outgrown last-click attribution and need sophisticated analytics to guide their next phase of growth.
